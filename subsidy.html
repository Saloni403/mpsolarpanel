Know About Your Subsidy

Discover the benefits of the MP Solar Tech Subsidy Program, designed to make solar energy affordable and accessible for homeowners and businesses.

Solar subsidy helps you adopt clean energy with ease while contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.

Benefits of Solar Subsidy:

  • Financial assistance that reduces installation cost
  • Lower initial investment for solar systems
  • Higher monthly savings on electricity bills
  • Supports government renewable energy schemes

Contact MP Solar Tech today to know your eligible subsidy amount and start your journey toward clean, affordable, and sustainable energy.

Government Solar Subsidy

Apply for a solar loan easily through SBI Bank. Under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, a subsidy of up to ₹78,000 is transferred directly to your bank account. MP Solar Tech provides complete support from application to installation, making it fast and hassle-free to get rooftop solar at your home.

Government subsidies vary based on the solar system size: • ₹30,000 for 1KW systems • ₹60,000 for 2KW systems • ₹78,000 for systems ranging from 3KW to 10KW MP Solar Tech handles all documentation to help you claim these benefits easily.

Under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, a 3KW rooftop solar power plant typically costs ₹2,00,000. With a margin money of ₹20,000 and a bank loan of ₹1,80,000, the government subsidy of ₹78,000 reduces your effective loan to just ₹1,02,000. This makes installing solar panels affordable while reducing your electricity bills.

Installing rooftop solar panels not only saves you money on monthly electricity bills, but also contributes to a cleaner environment by reducing carbon emissions. MP Solar Tech ensures professional installation and helps you maximize energy efficiency.

MP Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana it provides a simple and fast solution to install rooftop solar at home. Subsidy up to ₹78,000 is directly credited, reducing your bank loan burden. MP Solar Tech takes care of complete installation, paperwork, and bank processing to make the process smooth.
