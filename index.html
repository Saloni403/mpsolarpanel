Off-Grid Solar System

Off-grid solar systems operate independently from the main electricity grid, making them the best choice for remote locations or areas with frequent power cuts. These systems store solar energy in batteries and ensure 24/7 electricity availability. They provide complete energy independence and help reduce long-term electricity expenses while promoting clean and sustainable energy usage.

On-Grid Solar System

On-grid solar systems connect directly with the government electricity grid. These systems allow you to save heavily on electricity bills and even earn from excess power through net-metering. They are ideal for homes and businesses with steady grid supply and offer the highest financial savings compared to other types of solar systems.

Hybrid Solar System

Hybrid solar systems combine the benefits of both on-grid and off-grid technologies. They stay connected to the grid while also storing extra energy in batteries. This ensures uninterrupted electricity even during power failures. Hybrid systems are perfect for locations where grid power is available but unreliable.
