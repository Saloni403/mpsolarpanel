Rooftop Solar Panel

Rooftop solar systems are installed on commercial building rooftops and can be easily integrated with the existing electrical setup. They are cost-effective because net-metering reduces electricity bills. Installation is quick and unused rooftop space is efficiently utilized to generate clean and sustainable energy.

Ground Mount Solar

Ground-mounted solar systems are installed on open land where angle, direction, and spacing can be optimized for maximum energy production. Ideal for large commercial & industrial projects with higher power output, easy maintenance, and future expandability.
